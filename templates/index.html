The Flag Room

  • Quiet: The flag room is the perfect quiet room to study in. It also provides very soothing music from the piano played by students.
  • Vibes: Students use the piano often to play soft tunes that will provide a relaxing time to either study or nap in the comfortable couches.
  • Ambience: The flag room offers a great ambiance for students trying to study or do their assignments. The soft lightning, sound, and overall atmosphere make it a great place to hang out.
